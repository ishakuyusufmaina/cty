Even and Odd Numbers
CHAPTER 1

A number is even if it is divisible by 2 without a remainder. The modulo operator % can be used to find out if a number is even or odd.

Example 1.8
>>> 5 % 2 1
>>> # 5 is odd
>>> 6 % 2 0
>>> # 6 is even
>>> 3 % 2 1
>>> # 3 is odd
